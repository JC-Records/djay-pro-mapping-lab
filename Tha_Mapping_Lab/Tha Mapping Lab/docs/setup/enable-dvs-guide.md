# Enabling DVS in djay pro

## Requirements

- djay pro subscription with DVS support.
- DVS capable audio interface or mixer.
- Timecode vinyl or timecode signal.

## Steps

1. Open **djay pro**.
2. Go to **Settings > Audio Device Setup**.
3. Enable **DVS Mode**.
4. Assign the correct inputs to Deck 1 and Deck 2.
5. Play your timecode signal and open the calibration view.
6. Adjust input gain until the calibration circle is solid and stable.

Tips:

- Use good quality RCA cables.
- Keep input levels around -3 dB.
- Avoid running through extra mixers or processors if possible.
